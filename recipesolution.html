Perfect Fried Eggs

Learn how to make fried eggs with crispy edges and runny yolks. This simple yet delicious breakfast staple is ready in minutes and requires just a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of oil or butter
  • A pinch of salt
  • Fresh pepper
  • Optional spices like Allspice, parsley, thyme

Instructions

  1. Heat your frying pan over medium heat
  2. Add the oil or butter and wait until it's hot
  3. Crack eggs carefully into pan
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  5. Cook until egg whites are completely set but yolks are still runny, this should take 2 to 3 minutes
  6. Carefully remove eggs from pan with a spatula
  7. Garnish with the desired amount of spices and serve immediately
